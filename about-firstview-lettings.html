    About Firstview Lettings

    Since 2004 we've been providing residential letting mangement services to landlords and tenants in the South West of England. In this time we've helped landlords find their dream tenants, tenants find their dream landlords and managed letting management associations.

    More recently our focus has turned to serving the beautiful rural areas of Notinghamshire and Derbyshire, using the experience we've gained in the last 14 years.

    We're well versed in the letting process, for both sides, and recognize how difficult it can be. Our mission is to make the letting process simple and clear. We've established clear processes from start to finish allowing tenants know exactly where they are in the process at all times. Due to our stature of small but mighty, we're able to be hands on and attentive. Ensuring you dont slip through the net and your needs are met. (No more being passed from person to person!)

    Recognizing that not all landlords and tenants have the same needs is one of our greatest assets. We will work with you to identify your needs, whether thats location, accesibility, or something else as a tenant. If you're a landlord looking for new tenants or experienced property management, you've come to the right place.
