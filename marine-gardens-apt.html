    About this property

    Enjoy a high standard of living with this modern one-bedroom apartment located in the peaceful town of Coleford. A short walk from coleford town center, with access to supermarkets, restaurants, a golf club and lesuire center. A double bedroom, large lounge and kitchen area provide a comfortable amount of living space. Neutrally decorated and with double glazing and electric shower. Energy efficient Economy7 heating with night storage heaters ensures a warm home all year round. Larger towns are a short drive away and the A48 and M4/5 motorways are easily accesible.

    Local Services & Amenities

    Marine Gardens is a small block of apartment flats situated on a village road just outside Coleford town centre. Supermarkets, golf courses, a cinema and a leisure centre are all located close by. A short journey from Coleford are the larger Wye Valley towns of Ross and Monmouth. The main A48 trunk road and the M4/M5 motorways at Chepstow and Gloucester are easily accesible.
