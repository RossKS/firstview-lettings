    Lakeview Apartments - Fishponds - Bristol

    One bedroom 2nd floor flat in Fishponds, Bristol, BS32. Junction 2 M32, 1 mile from city centre Availability: Currently Let

    Description

    • Modern apartment, built approx 1996 Intercom entry system.
    • Large lounge with separate kitchen with fitted hob and built-in oven.
    • Double bedroom.
    • Bathroom including electric shower.
    • Economy7 heating with night storage heaters.
    • Council tax band B.

    Accommodation Dimensions

    • Lounge/Diner 14'5" x 11'9" (4.4m x 3.6m)
    • Bedroom 10'9" x 8'8" (3.28m x 2.64m)
    • Kitchen/Breakfast Room 11'8" x 7'2" (3.56m x 2.2m)

    Conditions

    The apartment is let unfurnished initially on a 6 month fixed term Assured Shorthold Tenancy Agreement. (copy available on request).

    Satisfactory references and credit checks required. Monthly rent payable by standing order monthly in advance. Deposit required as security against damages.

    Local Services & Amenities:

    Lakeview apartments are situated within walking distance of the busy Fishponds shopping area. Tesco supermarket, petrol station and Ikea store is 1 mile away on junction 2 of the M32. This junction is also the access point to the M5/M4 motorway network just 2 miles away. The Cribbs Causeway shopping centre, which includes restaurants and multi theatre cinema complex is within 30mins drive on junction 16 of the M5.
